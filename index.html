What is Idols of Ash?

Idols of Ash is an atmospheric descent horror experience built around pressure, movement, and survival. This page exists to give search engines and visitors a clear, readable, content-rich introduction before they continue to the official destination.

Why this GitHub page exists

A blank redirect page is weak for search and weak for users. This version uses proper metadata, structured content, and a prominent call to action so the page is useful on its own while still directing visitors to idolsofash.live.
